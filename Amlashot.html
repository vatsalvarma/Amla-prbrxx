What’s Special About This Drink?

Amla, also known as Indian gooseberry, has been a cornerstone of traditional wellness for centuries. Naturally rich and vibrant in flavor, it’s valued for its revitalizing and balancing qualities.

When combined with ginger for warmth and honey for smoothness, this shot becomes both refreshing and powerful. Shake & Gulp Amla Shot delivers this time-honored ingredient in a modern, easy-to-enjoy format. Since natural extracts settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.
